Bihar — Where History Breathes

Bihar is one of India's oldest and most historically significant states, located in eastern India. With a civilisation spanning over 3,000 years, it has been the birthplace of two world religions — Buddhism and Jainism — and the seat of the mighty Mauryan and Gupta empires.

From the ruins of Nalanda University to the sacred Bodhi Tree at Bodh Gaya, Bihar invites you to walk through living history.
